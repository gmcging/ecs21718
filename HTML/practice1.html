Ayaan Hirsi Ali

Award-Winning Activist

She was the founder of the AHA foundation for the defense of women's rights

She has received 17 awards for what she has done, and was named one of the 100 most influential people by Time magazine in 2005.

She was elected to the House of Representatives for the Netherlands in 2003.

Ayaan Hirsi Ali Wikipedia Page and Picture
